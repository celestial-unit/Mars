ENERGY SCALING ANALYSIS

Physics-Based Power Requirements

⚡ ENERGY SCALING PRINCIPLE

Stored magnetic energy scales with B²:

E ∼ B²

For a 10 m radius coil:

0.5 T → ~0.42 GJ (~116 kWh)

2 T → ~6.7 GJ (~1,860 kWh)

✅ That's 16× more energy than 0.5 T, but still orders of magnitude below 10 T (~46,000 MWh).

This is achievable with a small nuclear reactor + battery/SMES hybrid system.

🛡️ PROTECTION CAPABILITY BY FIELD STRENGTH

0.5 T Field

✓ Deflects most SEP protons (<200 MeV)
✓ Reduces acute dose during solar storms
✓ Minimal chronic protection

1–2 T Field

✓ Affects higher-energy SEPs (hundreds of MeV)
✓ Begins impacting lower-energy GCRs (GeV range)
✓ Provides meaningful partial chronic exposure reduction

Note: Full GCR protection is impossible for hab-scale volumes with current technology, but partial reduction is highly valuable.

🤖 AI OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES

⏱️ Predictive Operation

Use solar wind data to forecast SEP arrival. Only ramp field when storm is imminent, not continuously.

🔋 Energy Scheduling

Slow pre-charge using renewables + nuclear base load during low-demand periods. Burst to 2 T during critical windows.

🔌 Staged Control

Divide coil into segments; energize only needed sections based on storm intensity and exposure areas.

❄️ Cryogenic Optimization

Keep superconductor temperature just above minimum needed, reducing continuous cooling energy.
